Hi, I'm /rishikesh/█

This is my webspace. A niche-less blog where I gather and share my ideas and document my life. I consider this blog as an evolving art project itself.

I'm currently looking for exciting opportunities to work, spending most of my time applying for jobs. In parallel, I'm trying to write better, learning to code, working on a few side projects, focusing on my fitness, and trying to be a better husband.

I also write a newsletter read by around 3000 people across 93 countries and 48 US states.

The archive holds all the articles I've written, with Man from the Future being the most interesting and popular one so far. On this blog, I journal random things, track the books I read, movies & shows I watch, a dream list of things I want to do, the things that I use, and a collection of ideas.

I also host a puzzle, that I intend to run forever.

To see what I'm currently up to, check out the now page. For more about me, visit the about page or say hi!
